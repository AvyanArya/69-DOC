Pro Knowledge

The Wolf's Playbook

01

Tone Over Words

38% of communication is tone. You can say "I need to check my calendar" in a way that makes someone feel valued or dismissed. Train your tone daily.

02

Create the Need First

Never pitch features. Ask questions that surface the pain. The pen isn't a pen — it's the contract they can't sign without it.

03

Silence is Power

After you close, shut up. The next person to speak loses. Sit in the silence. Amateurs fill it with noise.

04

Match & Lead

Mirror their energy first, then slowly pull them to yours. If they're slow, start slow. Then accelerate. They'll follow without knowing it.

05

The Pen Drill

Hold a pen horizontally between your teeth. Read sentences aloud for 5 minutes daily. It forces exaggerated articulation and removes mumbling fast.

06

Record Yourself

Most people have never heard themselves speak. Record a 2-minute pitch. Watch it back. The cringe is your roadmap.
